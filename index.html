Our Story

Chris' Vegan Bakery started as a passion project in our home kitchen. What began with a single sourdough starter has grown into a beloved local bakery specializing in naturally leavened breads and decadent cookies.

We use only the finest plant based ingredients and traditional baking methods to create products that are as wholesome as they are delicious.
